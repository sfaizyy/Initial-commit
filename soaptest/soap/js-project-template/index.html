About us.


It all started in 2018 in my humble backyard where I use soapmaking as an escape from the harshness of reality.
Over time, I've accumulated soaps more than my family and I could ever need.
I founded Joie & Jude to share my creativity with the world
and to inspire others to pursue their dreams.

With our core values in mind,
we hope our products bring joy to many people around the world.
